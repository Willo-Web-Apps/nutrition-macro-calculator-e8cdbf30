# Design DNA — Somatix

## Visual Personality: bold-energetic
## Layout: product-led (hero calculator + feature deep-dives + CTA)
## Typography: bold-display

### Color Palette
- Primary: #1B4332 — deep forest green (oklch ~0.25 0.08 150)
- Accent: #F59E0B — bold amber/gold (oklch ~0.75 0.18 75)
- Surface-base: #0D1F17 — near-black green (oklch ~0.13 0.04 150)
- Surface-card: #162E23 — forest green card
- Text: #FDFAF5 — warm white (oklch ~0.99 0.005 80)

### Font Pairing
- Display: Archivo Black — weight 900 (hero headlines, huge numbers, section titles)
- Body: Barlow — weight 400/500/600 (UI labels, body copy, inputs, results)

### Unique Design Choices
1. **Warm dark theme** — Forest green base (#0D1F17 → #1B4332) instead of cold tech-blue dark; amber glows replace the typical purple/blue accent — feels premium, not clinical
2. **Calculator-as-hero** — The macro calculator is the centerpiece of the page, not a screenshot/mockup. Full-bleed dark green hero with a prominent card-widget featuring inputs and real-time result panels with oversized number typography
3. **Phase color coding** — Bulk / Cut / Maintain cards each have distinct amber/green/neutral color coding with large numeric outputs; result numbers rendered at display scale (text-5xl) to create visual impact
4. **Noise grain overlay** — SVG feTurbulence noise at 3% opacity over the hero gradient, making the background feel textured and handcrafted vs. flat digital
5. **Asymmetric section dividers** — Feature sections use a diagonal clip-path cut between dark green and near-black backgrounds, creating energy and movement between sections
